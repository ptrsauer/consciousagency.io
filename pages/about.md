# About

Research, experiments, and reflections by **Peter Sauer** — on AI agent orchestration, systems thinking, and what it takes to work well with machines that work.

I lead platform technology at a fintech in Berlin and run a personal research lab of 100+ AI agents that manage my knowledge, my calendar, and occasionally my training plan. This site is where the transferable parts of that work get written up.

Everything here is published for humans and machines alike: every post has a [markdown version](/llms.txt), the [RSS feed](/feed.xml) carries full text, and the whole site is a couple of hundred lines of Python away from its sources.
